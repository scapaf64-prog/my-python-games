

"Space Invaders"


Heavy shooter-action... 

ARROW-KEY's to move,    SPACE for shooting,    ESC for exit

3 eliminated enemies to gain one shield-point,     

If your shield is used up, your ship is red blinking and explode.

Light-brown enemies needs 3 hits, dark-brown enemies needs 2 hits and red enemies need only one hit to be destroyed.

When your ship is hit by a bullet, you are "invinvible" as long as your ship is blinking.
