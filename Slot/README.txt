
"Slot Machine"


SPACE to start the Machine,   Esc for exit.

By the way, the first three games after a win and the first five after a jackpot are duds.

There are also two cheat buttons:

"z" to trigger a win,   "d" to trigger a jackpot.  (For the impatient)

The game isn't completely polished, but it still plays smoothly.
